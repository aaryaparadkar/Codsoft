About Us

Hair Day is a boutique hair salon that offers expert cut, color, and styling services. Our salon also provides makeup and nail services to help you feel and look your best. We take pride in our warm and relaxing atmosphere, providing a "treat yourself" environment for our clients.

We are best known for our edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts, thanks to the expertise of our owner Pria and her good friend and talented hairdresser Garry. With a team of seven skilled professionals, including two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist, and one makeup artist, we ensure that our clients receive personalized and exceptional services.
